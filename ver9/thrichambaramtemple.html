Trichambaram Sree Krishna Temple

About the Temple

The main deity of the temple is Krishna after "Kamsavadham" sitting in His "Raudra" posture (ferocious posture). The Temple is among the Abhimana Kshethrams which is a list of hoary Vaishnavite shrines
